Mission

We aim to redefine charitable giving with unparalleled pragmatism, transparency, security, and dependability.

Solution

Like a wedding registry, charities use NeedList.ORG to list needed goods and services ("NeedLists") from suppliers.

How It Works

  1. Charities make NeedLists of specific items and quantities they need.
  2. NeedList.ORG collects and restricts donations to only buy/deliver approved items; issues donors tax receipts.
  3. Upon delivery, charities share short videos to thank and show donors their impact, strengthening connections.

Outcomes

Charities

Charities get what they need without the usual logistics, marketing, and admin hassles.

Donors

Donors get full transparency on how funds are used, skyrocketing trust.

Suppliers

Suppliers get recognition for supporting charities, earning favor from donors.

Case Study

Dec 12, 2024

• NeedList.ORG demoed the first "alpha test" NeedList with the St. Felix Centre (a Toronto homelessness charity) at a holiday party.

• $562 of winter clothes for the homeless was fully-funded that night by 20 strangers.

• We collected donations, issued tax receipts, and delivered items.
